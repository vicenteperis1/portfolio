WORDYSEE

Medical serious game for speech rehabilitation developed in collaboration with Vithas hospitals and Irenea Institute. This serious game allows patients to progress in a gamified way, where they will enjoy a space experience while recovering their language skills.

Role Unity Developer / UX Engineer
Stack Unity · C#
Year 2023
Partner LabLeni · Vithas · Irenea

14 Minigames

The game features 14 mini-games designed to target various speech therapy goals, created in collaboration with speech therapists. Each mini-game adapts its difficulty based on the patient’s progress, ensuring a personalized rehabilitation experience.

Progress Visualization

The application includes a comprehensive progress tracking system that records patients' performance in each mini-game. Speech therapists can access detailed reports and analytics to monitor improvements and adjust therapy plans accordingly.
