THE BASEMENT VR

A demon is in your basement, and you must exorcise it before it's too late! Pay attention to the signs that occur throughout the basement, and review your exorcism book for what to do in each situation to neutralize the demon's power.

Role Unity Developer / Game designer
Stack Unity · C#
Year 2022
Platform Meta Quest · Android

Gameplay

Complete the tasks in a timely and successful manner, or the demon will gain strength and eventually take over your body! Use your VR controllers to interact with objects.

Basement

The basement is filled with various objects and areas that the player must interact with to complete the exorcism tasks. Each element adds to the eerie atmosphere of the game.
